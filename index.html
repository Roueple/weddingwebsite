Our Journey

Our Love Story

A beautiful journey that began in high school and continues forever.

It all began with young love and a promise that lasted through the years...

We are high school sweethearts who first crossed paths during our teenage years. Around October-November 2012, what started as friendship soon blossomed into something more meaningful. On 10 November 2012 (101112), we officially began our journey together as a couple.

Through almost 13 years together, we've shared countless memories, grown together, and built a love that has only gotten stronger with time. Every day has been a key moment in our relationship, filled with laughter, support, and endless love.

Then, on 31 May 2024, during a romantic candlelight dinner at Melia Hotel, Gege asked the most important question, and of course, Kim said "Yes!"

We are incredibly excited to begin this new chapter as husband and wife, and we can't wait to share our special day with all of you, our dearest friends and family. Join us as we celebrate our love and the beginning of our forever!
